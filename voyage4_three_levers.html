Lever 1 — Shared embedding space
Which model embeds your queries?
Documents always embedded with voyage-4-large — queries use your chosen model. Shared space means they still compare correctly.
Lever 2 — MRL (Matryoshka Representation Learning)
How many dimensions do you keep?
Truncate the same vector — no re-embedding needed. Lower dims = smaller index, faster ANN search.
Lever 3 — Quantization
What numeric precision for storage?
Quantization-aware training minimises accuracy loss. Binary cuts storage by 32x vs float32.
